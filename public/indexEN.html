About US

FEEK is a web-based interactive app to collect feedback for e-commerce companies. 2 days after customers receive products they ordered from e-commerce companies, a notification pop-up for feedback will appear when they open the Internet browser. It’s not a boring form which is full of words but an exciting interface of a cute tree called Feek. Based on the way customers treat the tree, the center system will process treatment into data which could tell how they evaluate their experiences.

Why we do...

Among customers who are unsatisfied with the experiences, only 4% give feedback to the companies, 96% will never come back because they feel angry and do not want to waste time. Also, by reducing 5% people leaving, the revenue could increase by 50%. These analyses show the importance of customer feedback to a business which is often hard to collect.

Realize this important fact, we build FEEK as a smart solution to get feedback easily within a short time.
